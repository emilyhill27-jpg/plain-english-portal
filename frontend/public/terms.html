Terms of Use

Last updated: May 2026

What Plainly does

Plainly helps organisations make approved forms, letters and agreements easier to understand in plain English. It is not a substitute for legal, financial, or professional advice.

Your documents

You own your documents. When you upload a document to Plainly, we process it to generate a plain-English version. We do not store, copy, or retain your documents after your session ends.

Accuracy

Plainly uses AI to simplify documents. While we work hard to make the output accurate and helpful, AI can make mistakes. Always check the original document for anything important — especially legal terms, deadlines, or financial amounts. Plainly's output is a guide, not a replacement for the original.

End-user access

Access to Plainly is provided through participating organisations. End users do not need to create an account or pay to use the service. Use is limited to the approved document types chosen by the organisation. Do not use Plainly to process documents you do not have permission to access.

Organisation licences

Organisations using Plainly under an organisation licence are bound by a separate agreement. Contact us at hello@tryplainly.co.nz for details.

Changes

We may update these terms from time to time. We'll note the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Plainly means you accept the updated terms.
