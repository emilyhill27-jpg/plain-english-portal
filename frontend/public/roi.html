Context

What these numbers mean.

Clearer documents reduce the volume of calls that exist only because the original was hard to understand.

  • People who understand a letter or form the first time do not need to call to ask what it means.
  • Fewer confusion calls means shorter queues and faster service for people with genuine queries.
  • Staff spend less time repeating the same explanations and more time on work that matters.
  • Organisations that track call reasons can measure the change directly.
