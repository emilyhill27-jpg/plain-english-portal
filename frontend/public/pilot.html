Partner with Plainly

We're looking for one organisation to pilot Plainly — free for a term, in exchange for honest feedback and a case study.

Who we're looking for

We want to work with organisations that deal with complex documents every day — and care about making them clearer for the people who need them most.

  • Schools — SENCOs, learning support coordinators, or principals who want to make school communications accessible for every family.
  • Community organisations — Citizens Advice, community law centres, budgeting services, or any group whose clients struggle with complex paperwork.
  • Local councils — communications teams mandated to use plain language in public consultations.
  • Property managers — tenancy agreements, inspection letters, and maintenance notices are some of the most confusing documents families deal with.

What you get

Free access to Plainly for one full term (or 3 months). Plainly-branded experience with optional co-branding. A training session for your team. Priority support throughout the pilot.

What we ask

Permission to use your logo on our site. A short testimonial at the end. Two feedback sessions (20 minutes each). Anonymised usage data to help us improve.

What success looks like

Users report documents are clearer and easier to follow. Measurable reduction in follow-up calls or queries. A case study we can both be proud of.

No risk

No contracts, no lock-in. If it doesn't work for you, we part ways — no hard feelings. We just want to prove this works.
