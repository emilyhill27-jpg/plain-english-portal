# MSD Exclusions

Things Plainly must NOT do when processing MSD documents:

1. **Never tell someone they are eligible or ineligible.** Only explain what the document says about eligibility criteria.
2. **Never calculate benefit amounts.** Only show the amounts stated in the document.
3. **Never advise someone to apply or not apply.** Only explain what the form or letter asks for.
4. **Never interpret medical conditions.** If a document mentions a health condition, explain what the document says about it without adding medical commentary.
5. **Never guess what "discretionary" means in a specific case.** If a decision is described as discretionary, say that the decision is up to Work and Income to make.
6. **Never suggest how to answer a question.** Explain what the question is asking, not what to write.
